Search

PRmax enables fast efficient targeting of the entire UK media. Searches can be made for publications stations and individual journalists and Freelancers, by Media Channel, Subject, Geographic coverage, profile, interests or a combination of those factors allowing extreme precision

Save

The contents of each search can be viewed, edited and individual selections made simply by ticking publications or Journalists to be included. The results of one or several searches can then be saved to and kept updated on a stored distribution list

Distribute

The contents of any list can then be used to distribute directly from PRmax by HTML or Rich Text email enabling full use of content links with accompanying collateral such as photos, videos or supplementary documents, used to generate mailing labels or exported to generate mail merge files.
