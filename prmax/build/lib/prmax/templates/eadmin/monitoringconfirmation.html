It is a legal requirement to have the permission of the copyright owner to reproduce their work. To receive a monitoring service you may need a license from the Newspaper Licensing Agency (NLA) or the Copyright Licensing Agency (CLA). Your monitoring service provider is obliged to inform the relevant agency of their monitoring customers so you may be approached by a licensing agency and asked to take out the appropriate licence.
